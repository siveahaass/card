How To

  • Start Game

    Just start typing; the timer starts as soon as you begin typing!

    Click 'Next Game' or press 'Shift-Enter' to restart the game.

  • Scoring

    Every guess (that is a word) earns points for having correct letters

    Correct letters in the correct location receive twice as many points

    Every square left after correctly guessing the word counts as a correct letter

  • Play

    Guess the word by typing out guesses and pressing "Enter".

    Green = Correct letter and correct location.

    Orange = Correct letter but wrong location.

    Guesses do not have to be actual words (unlike the original Wordle game).

    Guesses that are words receives a hint of a correct letter in the correct position.

    Hit "Enter" to reset for the next word to guess (after guessing word or failing to do so)

    This game is based on the popular word game "Wordle" - with a typing twist!

Game Features

  • Restart Game Hotkey

    Keep your hands on the keyboard - use keyboard shortcut "Shift-Return" to restart game.

  • Multiple Word Lengths

    Try Twordle with different word lengths! 4 letter, classic 5 letters, 6 letter, and for an extra challenge 7 letters!

  • Live Statistics

    Shows your typing speed as you play.

  • High Scores

    Compete against yourself and other players with a high score board!

    Scores only last for 3 days to keep the competition fresh and to discourage cheating.
